Type 1 Hypervisor / Baremetal Hypervisor: 
The hypervisor runs directly on top of the physical servers. There is no servers in between. Mostly cloud providers uses them.


Type 2 Hypervisor / Hosted Hypervisor:
The hypervisor runs on top of the layer of Host OS that sits between physical server and the hypervisor.

They are used for the end-user virtualization.

E.g. Oracle, VMWare Workstation, VirtualBox.

They have higher latency than the type 1 hypervisor.

Benefits:
1. Cost Savings
2. Agility and Speed
3. Lower Downtime
